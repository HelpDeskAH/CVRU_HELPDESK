About Dr. Murari Lal Gaur

I am Dr. Murari Lal Gaur

At the helm of Dr. C V Raman University, my role as Vice Chancellor encompasses the strategic expansion of agricultural education, emphasizing the integration of hydrological science and watershed management. With expertise in environmental impact assessment, groundwater, and hydraulics, I guide our institution through innovative research and skill development programs, including a successful 200-hour training on micro-irrigation, in alignment with the Prime Minister's Skill India Mission.

Collaborating closely with ICAR-ATARI Pune and other stakeholders, we have fostered academic excellence and practical skill acquisition across various agricultural disciplines. Our team's dedication to advancing skill and livelihood prospects has positioned us as a pivotal force in agricultural education, continually shaping a robust curriculum that addresses contemporary challenges in resource management and sustains the agricultural sector's growth.
