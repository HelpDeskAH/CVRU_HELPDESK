About Anand Kumar

I am Anand Kumar

Head of the Department (HOD) and an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Dr. C.V. Raman University, Vaishali, Bihar. He holds an M.Tech in Thermal Engineering from Rabindranath Tagore University, Bhopal, and a B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering from Chouksey Engineering College, Bilaspur.

With over seven years of teaching experience, he has guided B.Tech projects on electric vehicles and B.O.T machines. He also has industrial experience as a G.E.T. Engineer at Atharva Foundry Pvt. Ltd., Pune. Anand has published research on improving the thermal performance of solar photovoltaic panels and actively participates in faculty development programs.

His technical skills include AutoCAD, and he is known for his adaptability, innovative thinking, and commitment to education and research.

About Dr. Nitish Sinha

I am Dr. Nitish Sinha

Assistant Professor at Dr. C.V. Raman University, Vaishali, Bihar, specializing in Mechanical Engineering. He holds a Ph.D. and M.Tech in Mechanical Engineering from VNIT, Nagpur, and has over a decade of teaching and research experience. His areas of interest include tribology, design, and modeling of dynamic systems.

Dr. Sinha has published extensively in leading journals and has contributed to several book chapters and international conferences. He is skilled in MATLAB, Mathematica, and Excel and has held key administrative roles such as Head of the Mechanical Engineering Department and Dean of R&D.

A recipient of the MHRD GATE Fellowship, Dr. Sinha is also a reviewer for reputed scientific journals and actively participates in organizing workshops and conferences to promote advanced research and learning.

About Pushpam Kumar Sinha

I am Pushpam Kumar Sinha

Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Dr. C.V. Raman University, Vaishali, Bihar. He holds an M.Tech in Thermal Engineering from Bhabha Engineering Research Institute, Bhopal, and a B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering from Millia Institute of Technology, Bihar.

With over three years of teaching experience, including roles at Millia Polytechnic, Purnea, and Girija Devi Polytechnic College, Bhojpur, Pushpam specializes in mechanical engineering concepts and practical applications. His technical skills include Microsoft Office and CATIA V5.
