About Farha Khan

I am Farha Khan

I am Farha Khan, currently working as a Librarian and Assistant Professor Library and Information Science Department at Dr C V Raman University, Vaishali, Bihar. With 9 years of experience in the field of library science and academic support, There is a Library Assistant and a Library Attendant working with me in the library staff . I have developed a passion for assisting students and faculty in accessing and utilizing academic resources effectively. In addition to my role as a librarian, I also teach Library Classification and Cataloguing. I am deeply committed to fostering an environment of learning and collaboration within the academic community. I am skilled in various library automation tools such as E Granthalaya, SOUL, KOHA and Libsys. Cataloging and classification of books and other resources. Maintaining library databases and Managing library circulation (issuing and returning books) and more.
